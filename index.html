WHO WE ARE:

MARTH Club is a community of artists and learners who create art using code. We believe in the following principles:
  • Community Support: We learn. We create. We help each other to learn and create.
  • NewKidLove: We all start as beginners, and we never forget that. We strive to help others to grow by sharing our knowledge freely.
  • NoCodeSnobs: When someone asks for help, we meet them at their level. We understand that everyone learns differently and that we are all at different levels of coding expertise.
  • OpenSource: We share our creations openly so that others might learn from our work and build upon it.
